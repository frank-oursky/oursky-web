Pre-configured Extraction Models

Extraction models on general shopping receipts, identity cards and most of the official licenses in Hong Kong are already available.

Multiple OCR Engines Supported

FormExtractor leverages the power of existing OCR services to deliver accurate results. Google Vision API, Azure Computer Vision OCR and Tesseract are currently supported.

Intuitive Web Portal

Users can submit forms, configure extraction parameters, label fields, add and manage existing models on our decorated web-based portal.

Photo Pre-processing

Uploaded photos are passed a set of optimazation processes including light and contrast adjustment, skew correction and edge detection to boost the extraction accuracy.

Edges of FormExtractor

FormExtractor labels the fields and return them as a strucutred JSON.
Traditional OCR engines only locate fragments of text and yield a string combining all of them. No strucutre nor labelling.
FormExtractor provides a web-based portal. All authorized parties can define the position and semantic of a form field, and tweak with the parameters and settings effortlessly.
Traditional ones only allow customization and management via complex code.

Use Cases

Shopping Receipts Loyalty Programme

Running loyalty programmes is a very common way for property developers to retain customers. A lot of these programmes reward cusomters with loyalty points based on their spendings which can be deduced from shopping receipts.

By integrating FormExtractor, the entire loyalty point distribution flow can become fully automatic. A customer can enter his/her account credentials and scan receipts at a scanner kiosk, where points will be calculated based on the extracted receipt data and sent to the user's loyalty account.

Insurance Claim Application

Even when it is done online, filing an insurance claim requires manual effort from both the company and client side. Staffs of the former will have to cross-check the form inputs against the submitted proofs. Meanwhile to submit a claim application a customer will have to convert the analogue data from the proofs, ensure the convertion process is flawless and attach scanned copies of proofs.

FormExtractor can relieve both sides from such tedious processes, only chore left is the copy scanning process. Cross-chcking time, human errors are all taken away!
