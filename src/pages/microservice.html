Skygear, our own serverless platform

Maintaining your own Microservices infrastructure isn’t easy: Selecting the right messaging protocol and managing user sessions across services is already a nightmare. Skygear is a serverless platform for web & mobile apps that lowers the bar to adopt Microservices.

Not only does Microservices improve the scalability and resilience of your applications, it also allows you to outsource server development work more easily when you are under-resourced as services are loosely coupled and communication between services are conducted over well-defined APIs.
